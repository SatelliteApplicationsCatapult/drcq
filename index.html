The Disaster Relief Coordination Questionnaire

DRCQ - Main Information

This is the Disaster Relief Coordination Questionnaire. You have been sent this link because a natural disaster has taken place in your area. The purpose of this webpage is to coordinate disaster relief locally: by filling out this questionnaire, if you are in need, we can send someone to help you. You can also provide assistance to people in need yourself.

The questionnaire will not take long and may help to save lives. Please fill in as much information as you can and we will try and get back in touch with you as soon as possible. Thank you for your time. Stay safe.

Injury Scale

Please tell us the severity of your injury using the number system below:

Level Severity of Injury
0 No injury
1 Very Slight - Does not require medical attention. Can be treated individually (e.g. mild headache)
2 Slight - Does not require medical attention. Can be treated individually (e.g. small cut)
3 Mild - May require medical attention at some point (e.g. muscular injury)
4 Medium - Requires medical attention at some point (e.g. shallow cut)
5 Serious - Requires medical attention (e.g. severe headache)
6 Very Serious - Requires immediate medical attention (e.g. deep cut, shallow breathing)
7 Extreme - In immediate danger. Requires immediate medical attention (e.g. heavy bleeding, head injury, weak pulse)
8 Very Extreme - Risk of death. Requires immediate medical attention (e.g. loss of limbs, unconsciousness, no breathing)
